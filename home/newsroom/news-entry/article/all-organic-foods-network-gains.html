all-Organic Foods Network Gains New Sponsorship

  • In food
  •  health
  • Posted15 Dec, 2015

all-Organic Foods Network, an organic food delivery network, has been slowly gaining visibility and new attention. Recently, it attracted the attention of a new sponsor - the Greater Parents’ Council of Happytown.

 

 

When asked why they are interested in supporting the all-Organic Foods Network, a spokesperson for the Greater Parents’ Council said they want every child to have quality food in their bellies. This has been shown to be a factor in doing well at school and leads to greater success in life.

 

 

“I believe spinach is good for everyone.” ~ Popeye the Sailor, parent to Sweet Pea

 

 

As a result of their sponsorship, the all-Organic Foods Network is receiving media attention and new funding from the network. These funds will be used to buy farming supplies, upgrade their technology and fund the distribution of an additional 60,000 pounds of food over the holiday season.


“No family will go hungry in Happytown for this holiday season, thanks to the generosity of our citizens.” ~ Bill Taylor, CEO of all-Organic Foods Network
