Daniel Taber
CEO (All-Movies)

As long as he can remember, Daniel Taber has loved movies and what they bring to people - joy, happiness and good times. Now he shares his passion for good films with people around the world through CoMovie Central. Known for his ability to inspire others, he has built a great team who have, collectively, served more than one million movies to happy audiences.
