CEOs of The Digital Roundtable

  • 27 janvier 2016
  •  Elara Hilton Las Vegas

A dynamic ‘meeting of the minds’ of the CEOs of our top companies, including Daniel Taber (CoMovie Central), Bill Taylor (Organi-Food Network) and Julie Hegebottom (Digitall).

Entrepreneurs, investors and stakeholders are invited to attend and learn from the CEOs who share in casual dialogue some of their most significant insights. After the roundtable, participants are encouraged to meet the CEOs personally.
